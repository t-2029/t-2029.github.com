Aspects to Consider

  1. Power adapters/converters
  2. Phone chargers
  3. Medication reglation
  4. Copies of necessary documentation
  5. Bank notification
  6. Passport being valid for at least 6 months
  7. Obtaining necessary visas


Packing lists vary depending on one's planned destination. It is highly important to keep in mind key considerations such as weather, laws/forbidden items (airport included), and the amount of days you'll be staying there as well as accessibility for cleanliness.






It is very common for travelers to not properly prepare for their trip. It is this website's mission to ensure that people can feel properly prepared as well as safe while traveling. For any more add-ons or questions, please contact us.
